Getting Started

This is a simple email application. Use the sidebar to navigate between folders, click an email to read it, and use the Compose button to write new messages.

Folders

  • Inbox — Received emails. Unread count shown in the sidebar.
  • Starred — Emails you've marked as important.
  • Sent — Emails you've sent.

Reading Email

Click any email in the list to open it. From the detail view you can:

  • Reply — Opens a compose window pre-filled with the original message.
  • Star / Unstar — Mark emails as important.
  • Delete — Remove the email.

Composing Email

Click the Compose button in the sidebar to write a new email. You can:

  • To field — Type a recipient address, or start typing to see a list of known contacts to pick from.
  • Send — Delivers the email (appears in your Sent folder).
  • Discard — Closes the compose window without saving.

Search

Use the search bar above the email list to filter emails by sender, subject, or content within the current folder.

Data Storage

All email data is stored in your browser's local storage. Clearing browser data will reset the app to its default sample emails.
